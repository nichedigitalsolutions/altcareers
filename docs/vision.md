# AltCareers Vision

## Mission
Help people discover career opportunities beyond traditional degree pathways through AI-powered, personalized guidance.

## Vision
Become the most trusted platform for discovering alternative careers, study pathways, and emerging opportunities.

## Target Users
- High school learners
- Matriculants
- University dropouts
- Career changers
- Job seekers

## Core Principles
- Opportunity over prestige
- Multiple pathways to success
- Data-driven recommendations
- Transparent AI
- Practical career planning
