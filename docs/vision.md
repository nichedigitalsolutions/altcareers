# AltCareers Vision

## Mission

Help people discover fulfilling career opportunities beyond traditional university pathways through AI-powered, personalized career guidance.

## Vision

To become the leading AI-powered career discovery platform that helps students, job seekers, and career changers explore alternative careers, qualifications, certifications, apprenticeships, and emerging industries.

## Problem

Traditional career guidance focuses on a small number of well-known careers such as doctors, lawyers, accountants, and engineers.

Millions of people are never exposed to high-demand alternative careers that may better suit their interests, personalities, budgets, or lifestyles.

Career information is scattered across universities, colleges, TVETs, certification providers, blogs, and government websites, making it difficult to compare options and make informed decisions.

## Solution

AltCareers brings everything together into one intelligent platform.

Users answer questions about themselves, and AltCareers recommends suitable careers, explains why they match, and provides clear pathways into those careers.
